Server Rules - Z X Server

1. Respect Everyone
- Treat other players and staff with courtesy. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, hate speech, or threats will not be tolerated.

2. No Cheating or Exploits
- Using hacked clients, mods that give unfair advantage, duping, or exploiting server bugs is prohibited. Report exploits to staff immediately.

3. No Griefing or Stealing
- Destroying, modifying, or taking another player's builds or items without explicit permission is forbidden. Respect land claims and protections.

4. PvP and Combat
- PvP is allowed only where explicitly enabled. Do not ambush newcomers, raid bases marked as safe, or attack players in spawn/shops unless rules allow it.

5. Builds and Lag
- Do not create contraptions that cause excessive lag (mass redstone, excessive entities, etc.). Staff may require modification or removal of laggy builds.

6. No Advertising or Spam
- Advertising other servers, websites, or services without permission is not allowed. Avoid repetitive messages, caps spam, or disruptive emotes.

7. Appropriate Content
- Keep chat and build content appropriate for a general audience. No pornographic, extremely violent, or otherwise offensive content.

8. No Impersonation
- Do not impersonate staff, other players, or public figures to deceive or manipulate others.

9. Economy & Trading
- Real-money trading, selling/buying accounts, or trading outside approved server systems without staff approval is prohibited.

10. Privacy & Doxxing
- Do not share or solicit personal information of others. Doxxing or threats of exposing private data will result in severe action.

11. Follow Staff Instructions
- Respect staff decisions. If you disagree with a staff action, use the appeal process rather than arguing in public.

12. Reporting
- Report rule violations via the designated method (in-game report system, Discord, or ticket). Provide evidence (screenshots/logs) when possible.

13. Ban Appeals
- Banned players may appeal through the official appeals channel. Appeals must be respectful and include relevant information.

14. Consequences
- Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, permanent bans, build rollback, or item removal depending on severity and history.

15. Rule Changes
- Rules may be updated. Players are responsible for staying informed of current rules.

By playing on this server you agree to follow these rules. Help keep the community safe and enjoyable for everyone.
